WimTVPro is the video plugin that adds several features to manage and publish video on demand, TV-style video schedules and stream live events on your website.

== Description ==

With WimTVPro you can extend WordPress with a powerful plugin that allows you to organize, store, publish and stream video in posts, pages or widgets to any location within your website/blog.

Storage and bandwidth used for the video is provided by WimTV, the innovative platform for live and on demand video streaming. To use the plugin, you must have a Web TV account on WimTV. Registration is free of charge. You can easily sign up directly from the plugin.

WimTVPro allows its users to:

*  upload and publish videos
*  for each video published, set of access conditions (free, creative commons, pay per view)
*  copy the iframe generated to post videos anywhere on the page (e.g. in posts, pages or widgets)
*  customize the logo of the video player
*  create a TV-style video schedules and insert into the pages and posts of the site
*  create live streaming events to be published on the pages of your site
*  recording video of live events that have been already broadcasted
*  control and monitor access analytics for your content
*  embed your live, schedules and on demand videos on any site
*  set a price for the visualisation of your on demand and live video content. Just connect your Web TV to your PayPal account
*  set your privacy to make videos available just on your website or on WimTV platform too
*  create a video channel on WimTV, becoming part of the community

The plugin is integrated into the WordPress menu and is divided in specific section, to always have an ordered control of content.
For more details about WimTVPro's functionalities visit: http://wimtvpro.tv

== Installation ==

1. Upload "WimTVPro" to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

Please check that your php server has the "php_curl" extension properly installed.

That's it! Enjoy your WimTVPro plugin.
